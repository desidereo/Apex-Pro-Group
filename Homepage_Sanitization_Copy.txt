
## 22. HOW TO FIND & EDIT THE FOOTER (Step-by-Step)
**Method 1: The "Edit with Elementor" Shortcut (Easiest)**
1.  Go to your actual website homepage (view it as a visitor).
2.  Look at the black bar at the very top of the screen (the Admin Bar).
3.  Hover over **"Edit with Elementor"**.
4.  A dropdown menu will appear. Look for **"Footer"** (or something like "Global Footer").
5.  Click that. It will take you straight to the footer editor.

**Method 2: The Dashboard Route**
1.  Go to your WordPress Dashboard.
2.  Look for **Templates** in the left sidebar (under "Elementor").
3.  Click **Theme Builder**.
4.  On the next screen, look for the **Footer** tab (usually on the left side).
5.  Click on your active footer (it might say "Global Footer" or "Main Footer").
6.  Click **Edit**.

**Method 3: The Customizer (If you don't use Elementor for the footer)**
1.  Go to **Appearance > Customize**.
2.  Look for **Footer Builder** or **Footer Widgets**.
