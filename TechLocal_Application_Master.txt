### Question 18: Risks
(Guidance: This is the final major question. We must show we have thought about what could go wrong.)

Answer:
We have identified the key risks and implemented a mitigation strategy for each.

Risk 1: Technical - Latency Targets Not Met (Rating: High)
- Description: The local GPU bridge might not be fast enough to beat cloud latency (<20ms).
- Mitigation: We have budgeted for "Over-Spec" hardware (RTX 4090s) to provide a performance buffer. If Python is too slow, we have reserved time in WP2 to rewrite critical path components in C++/Rust (Zero-copy memory access).

Risk 2: Recruitment - Failure to Hire Specialist Staff (Rating: Medium)
- Description: Difficulty finding 3 specialist staff (Systems Eng, Data Scientist, Operations & QA Lead) in Essex within 1 month.
- Mitigation: We have allocated a competitive salary budget. We will prioritize the Senior Systems Engineer hire first. If delays occur, we will hire a remote UK-based contractor temporarily while continuing the search for permanent local employees.

Risk 3: Commercial - Hardware Cost Inflation (Rating: Medium)
- Description: GPU prices fluctuate wildly due to AI demand.
- Mitigation: We have secured a quote from a UK supplier valid for 90 days. We also have a 10% contingency fund within the "Materials" budget to absorb price spikes.

Risk 4: Regulatory - AI Safety & GDPR (Rating: Low)
- Description: Handling financial data requires strict privacy compliance.
- Mitigation: This project is inherently "Privacy First" because all data is processed locally (offline). No customer data leaves the premises, making us naturally GDPR compliant. We will ensure the AI model is "non-generative" for financial advice (i.e., it provides analysis, not investment recommendations) to avoid FCA regulatory triggers.

Risk 5: Managerial - Project Overrun (Rating: Low)
- Description: R&D taking longer than 6 months.
- Mitigation: We are using Agile methodology with weekly sprints. If a specific feature (e.g., "Sentiment Analysis") is lagging, we will de-scope it to ensure the core "Latency Bridge" is delivered on time and within budget.
